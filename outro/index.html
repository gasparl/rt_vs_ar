Short info about this test:
The polygraph-based concealed information test is widely used in Japan. However, there is an alternative, response time based method, that is currently in development. This latter method has several practical advantages: it allows simpler testing (e.g., no special equipment), it is easier to evaluate, and it has been shown to be resistant to faking. However, so far there have been no studies that compared the polygraph and response time based tests in view of detection accuracy (i.e., how many people can the given methods correctly identify as concealing or not concealing given details). This is what we do in our experiment: Participants, who may or may not have taken part in a mock-crime, are tested with both methods, and in the end we will see how the two methods perform and whether either is better than the other.
For more details, please ask the experiment leader.
